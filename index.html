About Our Graduates

The Climate Lens

Climate isn't a sector. It's a lens, one that touches every industry, every domain, every function. Our graduates don't learn "climate" in isolation. They learn how to apply an interdisciplinary climate perspective to the problems they want to solve.

Climate and Society graduates are uniquely qualified to apply their interdisciplinary climate curriculum to any sector and function: from decarbonization and resilience to environmental health and socio-political change, with an orientation towards evidence-based decision making and equitable outcomes.

Students arrive with different professional and academic backgrounds, varying levels of experience, and distinct goals. There is no singular pathway. What unites them is the ability to see climate's intersection with everything, and the skills to act on it.

Graduates are adept at communicating the value and impact of climate work and are entrusted with building and stewarding the partnerships that accelerate climate action and progress.

The breadth of placements below is meant to inspire. These are real examples of how our graduates are working on climate and sustainability challenges across the economy.
